Research Contents

Research Theme
 Research theme: Compensation of non-linear phenomena in motion control applications by using switched disturbance observers

My research interest is mainly oriented to the compensation of non-linear phenomena in motion control applications, such as:
・ Non-linear friction in geared motors caused by oil seals
・ Backlash in geared motors
・ Contact motion of industrial robots during force control

My research work focuses on combining robust control methods such as disturbance observers (DOb) with switched control theory to achieve model-less robust solutions for geared motors and industrial robots with inherent nonlinearities.

Feel free to contact me if you have any questions regarding my research or if you wish to download any of my papers.
