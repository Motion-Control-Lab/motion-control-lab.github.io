Kiyoshi Ohishi




Biography

1981/3 B.S. degree of electrical engineering - Keio University, Yokohama, Japan
1983/3 M.S. degree of electrical engineering - Keio University, Yokohama, Japan
1986/3 Ph.D. degree of electrical engineering - Keio University, Yokohama, Japan
1986/4 Assistant Professor - Osaka Institute of Technology, Osaka, Japan
1989/4 Associate Professor - Osaka Institute of Technology, Osaka, Japan
1993/4 Associate Professor - Nagaoka University of Technology, Niigata, Japan
2003/8 Professor - Nagaoka University of Technology, Niigata, Japan

Awards

1986IEEE. IES. IECON'85 Outstanding Paper Award
1997 JSME Robotics and Mechatronics Division ROBOMEC Award
1998IEEJ Industry Applications Society Service Award
2001IEEJ Industry Applications Society Service Award


His research interests include mechatronics, robotics, and power electronics.
He is an administration comittee member of the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IEEE IES), the Institute of Electrical Engineers of Japan (IEEJ), the Robotics Society of Japan (RSJ), the Society of Instrument and Control Engineers (SICE), and the Japan Society of Mechanical Engineers (JSME).
